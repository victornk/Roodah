About

Roodah Bros Trading is a young growing dynamic organisation committed as a company to transformation and empowerment through ownership. Roodah Bros trading is committed to ensure that the customer receives the building material ordered, on time, and at a fair price. We represent reputable manufacturers who produce good quality building materials. The organization is very focused in combating poverty through acceleration of service delivery to the poor. Roodah Bros trading is the forefront of building science across all sectors and applications, with construction material you trust.

Mission Statement

Roodah bros trading is maintaining level one (1) BEE status contribution to sustainable development, growth and better life for all.
We offer personal services and a direct customer care to our clients.
We strongly believe in win to win relationship and value team work.
We are committed to continuous development.
